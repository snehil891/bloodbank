ABOUT US

AIM

This Blood Bank is an initiative by a group of GITAM B.Tech CSE students to provide the city with a solution for any issue related with Blood Donation/Acceptance. We aim at making this website reachable enough to anyone who's in need, instantly! GITAM Blood Bank is committed on providing the patients with an online portal where they can request for blood or take an appointment if volunteering for blood donation!

OBJECTIVE

With this facility being provided to the people, they will no more need to rush to the hospital for searching for blood or when volunteering up for blood donation. People can raise a request for either of the two and we will revert back as soon as possible.
